ABAP

ABAP (Allgemeiner Berichts Aufbereitungs Prozessor) means literally 'General Report-Consolidating Processor'
or 'Processador de Criação de Relatórios Gerais', also an acronym for 'Advanced Business Application Programming'.

ABAP is a programming language developed by SAP (Systeme, Anwendungen und Produkte in der Datenverarbeitung)
or 'Systems, Applications and Products in data processing'.

Different from other languages, ABAP was specifically developed to be used only in SAP systems.

SAP systems are used by enterprises to take care of all of its business related processes.

So essencialy when programming in ABAP you must have your mindset focused mainly on B2B processes.

There are many SAP products (e.g. CRM, BI, HR, PLM, ...) but the mostly known is 'SAP ERP' or 'SAP ECC':

SAP
ERP  (Enterprise Resource Planning)
ECC  (Enterprise Central Component)
X.X  (Version)
EhPX (Enhancement Package)
